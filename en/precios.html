Why are there no fixed prices?

The price of a project depends on many factors: complexity, estimated time, whether it's on-site or remote, if it includes subsequent support and what technologies are necessary. I prefer to give you an honest quote tailored to what you really need, instead of a generic price that doesn't fit. Always without commitment and with a quick response.

Do you work remotely or on-site?
add remove

Both. For on-site technical support, I travel across Tenerife (mainly South and Santa Cruz). For web development, apps, remote support, and infrastructure, I work from anywhere in the world without any problem. Often, resolving things remotely is more efficient.

How much does a website or app cost?
add remove

It depends on the scope of the project. A simple landing page doesn't cost the same as a web application with an admin panel, database, and AI integration. To give you a concrete figure, I need to know your needs.

What I can tell you: I work with budgets adapted to the reality of freelancers and SMEs. No inflated agency prices.

How does the monthly maintenance plan work?
add remove

The monthly maintenance plan covers continuous care for your web or app: security updates, verified backups, uptime monitoring, minor bug fixes, and small content or visual adjustments.

Each plan has a set number of monthly intervention hours agreed upon. Large changes (new features, redesigns) are outside the plan and quoted separately. The contract is monthly with no commitment.

There are modalities for static webs, webs with backend or CMS, and more complex web apps. Contact me so I can indicate what fits best in your case.
