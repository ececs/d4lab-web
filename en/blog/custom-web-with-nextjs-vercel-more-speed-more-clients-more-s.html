A Client's Challenge: Optimizing the Digital Experience

We recently worked with "Sol y Sabor," a charming café in southern Tenerife, renowned for its excellent coffee and relaxed atmosphere. Despite its local reputation, its online presence left much to be desired. Their old website, built on a generic platform, was slow, unintuitive, and didn't reflect the quality and charm of the establishment. This translated into:

  • Few online reservations.
  • Difficulty showcasing their updated menu.
  • Poor local search rankings.
  • A frustrating user experience, especially on mobile devices.
The Sol y Sabor team knew their website was their digital storefront, but it wasn't living up to its potential. They needed a solution that would not only enhance their image but also directly impact their business: more customers and, consequently, more sales.

The Solution: Next.js and Vercel to the Rescue

We perfectly understood their needs. For Sol y Sabor, and for many other businesses looking to stand out in Tenerife's competitive digital market, the answer lay in a custom-built website, powered by cutting-edge technologies. This is where the synergy between Next.js and Vercel comes into play.

What is Next.js and Why is it Key?

Next.js is a React framework that enables the creation of fast, scalable web applications with an exceptional developer experience. Unlike other solutions, Next.js is designed from the ground up for performance optimization and user experience.

  • Hybrid Rendering (SSR, SSG, ISR): Next.js offers flexibility to render pages on the server (SSR), statically generate them at build time (SSG), or even incrementally (ISR). This means your website loads almost instantly, regardless of its complexity or content. For Sol y Sabor, this translated into an instantly loading menu and a site that felt agile at all times.
  • Image Optimization: Heavy image loading is the enemy of speed. Next.js includes an image optimization component that resizes and serves images in modern, efficient formats, drastically improving load times – crucial for showcasing their delicious dishes.
  • File-based Routing: Simplifies route creation and code organization, allowing our team to develop more efficiently.
  • Integrated API Routes: Enables creating API endpoints directly within the Next.js project, facilitating integration with external services or building custom functionalities, like a simplified reservation system.

Vercel: The Perfect Home for Your Website

If Next.js is the engine, Vercel is the high-speed highway. Vercel is a deployment and hosting platform specifically optimized for Next.js applications. Its global infrastructure and focus on delivery speed make a massive difference.

  • Continuous Deployment: Every time we make a change or add a new feature, Vercel automatically deploys the updated version of the website without interruption.
  • Global CDN: Vercel uses a Content Delivery Network (CDN) distributed worldwide. This means your website is served from the server closest to your visitors, guaranteeing ultra-fast load times, no matter where they are. For Sol y Sabor, this ensured that both tourists from Las Palmas and locals from Adeje had a smooth experience.
  • Optimized Performance: Vercel is built to maximize the performance of Next.js applications, delivering exceptional Google Core Web Vitals metrics, which directly translate into better SEO.
  • Security and Scalability: The platform handles security and automatically scales according to traffic, freeing us from technical worries.

The Impact on Sol y Sabor: Speed, Clients, and Sales

The digital transformation of Sol y Sabor was remarkable. By migrating to a website built with Next.js and hosted on Vercel, the results were immediate:

  • Improved Load Speed: The site's pages, including the interactive menu and photo gallery, went from loading in several seconds to under one second. Google Core Web Vitals metrics showed significant improvement.
  • Increase in Organic Traffic: Performance optimization and a better user experience boosted Sol y Sabor's ranking in local Google searches. More people found the café when searching for "café in [their area]" or "best coffee Tenerife."
  • Rise in Online Reservations: The new website was intuitive and easy to use. The online reservation process was simplified, resulting in a 25% increase in website reservations within the first month.
  • Enhanced Conversion Rate: Visitors spent more time on the site, explored the menu and promotions, and were more inclined to visit the café or make a reservation. The bounce rate decreased drastically.
  • Flawless Mobile Experience: With a significant portion of traffic coming from mobile devices, the mobile-optimized web design of Next.js and Vercel ensured that every user, regardless of their device, had a positive and frictionless experience.
